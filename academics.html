Academic Programs

Our comprehensive academic programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging exploration of their interests and talents.

Primary Education (Grades 1-5)

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and essential life skills through interactive and engaging learning methods.

  • English and Native Language Development
  • Mathematics Fundamentals
  • Science Exploration
  • Creative Arts and Music
  • Physical Education

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Expanding knowledge and developing critical thinking skills across various subjects with specialized instruction.

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Science Laboratory Work
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science Basics
  • Foreign Language Options

High School (Grades 9-12)

Comprehensive preparation for higher education and career readiness with diverse subject choices.

  • Advanced Placement Courses
  • Science and Technology Programs
  • Humanities and Literature
  • Career Counseling
  • College Preparation

Special Programs

Additional opportunities for student growth and development beyond the standard curriculum.

  • Debate and Public Speaking
  • Science Olympiad
  • Mathematics Competition
  • Art and Music Clubs
  • Sports Teams
